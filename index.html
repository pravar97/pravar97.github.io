Our Story

FlexiWish sparked into existence at a dynamic university hackathon, fueled by a shared vision: to make cutting-edge health technology accessible and impactful. Our founding team, a blend of passionate Computer Science students and a dedicated Neurobiology and Aging PhD student, saw an opportunity to combine our diverse expertise to tackle real-world health challenges.

Driven by the desire to translate complex biomechanics and neurobiology into user-friendly solutions, we focused on creating a tool that empowers individuals to understand and improve their physical well-being. The initial concept, refined through intense collaboration and innovative problem-solving during the hackathon, laid the groundwork for what FlexiWish is today.

Our mission is to provide an intuitive, AI-powered platform that offers valuable insights into joint health and inflammation indicators. We are committed to helping you move better, feel better, and take proactive steps towards a healthier life, all stemming from that initial spark of creativity and a drive to innovate for good.

We continue to embody the spirit of that hackathon – agile, curious, and dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in health tech. Our journey is one of continuous learning and development, always focused on delivering meaningful results for our users.
